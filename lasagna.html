Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is made with pasta, cheese (a lot of it) and various sauces

It is often cooked using an oven

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Lightly cook lasagna noodles by dipping them in hot water
  2. Oil the oven dish
  3. Layer ingredients on top of each other and cook until brown
